Executive Summary

IslamicFamily is pleased to present this proposal for Fractional Controller services to Ummah Society. Having had the opportunity to understand your organization's current financial model, growth trajectory, and operational structure, we believe we are well-positioned to help Ummah Society navigate its next stage of development.

Ummah Society has demonstrated remarkable growth — revenue has increased 50% year-over-year and now requires financial leadership commensurate with that scale. We propose to serve as your embedded financial leadership: providing the controller-level oversight, high-level strategic guidance, and reporting infrastructure your organization needs to grow sustainably and serve your community with confidence.

🔍

Understanding Your Needs

Through our exploratory discussions, we identified the following key themes:

Strengths to Build On

  • Established systems for tracking revenue streams from donations, tuition, and grants
  • Effective use of Dext for AP, credit cards, and invoice management
  • Existing budgeting process for revenue and expenses
  • Strong budget controls enforced by organizational leadership

Gaps We Propose to Address

  • Financial literacy — translating numbers into insight and action for departmental managers and leadership
  • Capacity constraints — Treasurer Ahmed Nassrat needs to offload hands-on workload
  • Growth management — 50% YoY revenue demands more sophisticated forecasting and reporting
  • Transition from quarterly to monthly financial reconciliation
  • Capital budgeting — currently absent from the planning cycle
  • Audit support — guidance through the annual external audit
  • Aplos reporting optimization

Terms and Termination

Initial Term

This engagement is proposed for an initial term of twelve (12) months commencing on the onboarding date. Following the initial term, the engagement continues month-to-month unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Notice Period

Either party may terminate by providing 30 days' written notice after completion of Phase 1 (Month 3). A minimum three-month commitment applies during Phase 1 to ensure continuity of the foundation work.

Upon termination: IslamicFamily will deliver all financial records, working files, and Aplos documentation to Ummah Society within 10 business days, and will support a reasonable transition period not to exceed two weeks.
